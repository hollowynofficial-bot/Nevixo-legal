Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Welcome to DayFlow. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

Information We Collect

Depending on the features you use, DayFlow may collect and store:

  • Your email address (only if you create an account).
  • Your tasks, habits, notes, and application settings.
  • Your notification preferences.
  • Anonymous technical information necessary to keep the application working properly.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to:

  • save your progress;
  • synchronize your data across your devices;
  • provide notifications that you enable;
  • improve the reliability and quality of DayFlow.

Data Storage

Account data is securely stored using Supabase. Some settings may also be stored locally on your device to improve your experience.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your information is never shared with third parties for advertising purposes.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, DayFlow stores your notification preferences solely to deliver reminders that you have chosen. You can disable notifications at any time from your device settings or within the app.

Your Rights

You may:

  • access your personal data;
  • delete your account;
  • request removal of your stored information.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
